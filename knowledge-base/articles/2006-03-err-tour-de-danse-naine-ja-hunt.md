# Tour de danse: Naine ja hunt (Episode 75)

**ERR Tour de danse**
First broadcast: March 9, 2006
Host: Jaak Põldma
Director: Ruti Murusalu
Editor: Epp Ehand

Dance television program featuring coverage of multiple performances including ZUGA ühendatud tantsijate "Naine ja Hunt".

## Episode Content

This episode covered multiple dance events:

1. **"Sõduri lugu" (The Soldier's Tale)**

   - Venue: Vanemuine
   - Choreographer: Rachid Tika (Belgian choreographer)
   - Music: Igor Stravinsky

2. **"Undiin"**

   - Company: Dmitri Harchenko and dance theatre Zicki
   - Venue: Kanuti Gildi SAAL

3. **"Naine ja Hunt" (Woman and Wolf)**

   - Company: ZUGA ühendatud tantsijad
   - Venue: Kanuti Gildi SAAL

4. **"Dr. Tõdva ja professor Sule süsteem" (Dr. Tõdva and Professor Sule's System)**

   - Choreographers: Triin Lilleorg, Kärt Tõnisson
   - Venue: Kanuti Gildi SAAL

5. **Kaie Kõrbi book presentation**
   - Meeting with prima ballerina after her departure from stage

## Context

**Tour de danse** was ERR's television program dedicated to dance coverage, documenting both contemporary dance and ballet performances across Estonia. Episode 75 captured a particularly active period in Estonian dance with multiple premieres at Kanuti Gildi SAAL.

## Award Recognition

**"Naine ja Hunt" subsequently won the 2007 Tantsuauhind (Dance Award)**

From Estonian Theatre Union:

> **2007 ZUGA ühendatud tantsijad** – lavastuse „Naine ja Hunt" idee, koreograafia ja videorežii (Kanuti Gildi SAAL).

Award given for:

- Idea
- Choreography
- Video direction

This was a significant recognition for ZUGA's multimedia approach and cross-cultural collaboration with British artist Bush Hartshorn.

## Historical Context

**ZUGA's Award History:**

- **2005**: Tantsuauhind for "ZUGA teeb suure linna" (Kaja Kann, Helen Reitsnik, Tiina Mölder)
- **2007**: Tantsuauhind for "Naine ja Hunt"

"Naine ja Hunt" marked ZUGA's second major award in three years, establishing their position in Estonian contemporary dance.
