# Teatriluup: meeleKolu

**ERR Vikerraadio**
Broadcast: October 18, 2016
Host: Keiu Virro

Interview with ZUGA united dancers Kärt Tõnisson and Ajjar Ausma about the children's performance "meeleKolu".

## Context

Teatriluup is ERR Vikerraadio's theatre review program. This episode features an interview with two of ZUGA's dancers discussing "meeleKolu", a performance created for young children ("vaguratele mürsikutele ja avastavatele kainikutele") and their families.

## Production Details

**meeleKolu** premiered in 2016, continuing ZUGA's tradition of creating participatory contemporary dance performances for children.

## Related Coverage

Keiu Virro also wrote a review of "meeleKolu" for Eesti Päevaleht on the same date (18. oktoober 2016): "meeleKolu, lastele mõeldud tantsulavastus, mis ei küsi vanust"
