# Laste lood: Võluvärk

**ERR Vikerraadio**
Broadcast: November 22, 2011
Author and Host: Kadri Tiisel

Radio program "Laste lood" (Children's Stories) featuring ZUGA's performance "Võluvärk" (Magic Stuff).

Students from Tallinna Toomklubi Teatrikool (Tallinn Dome Club Theatre School) - Heliis Kuulman and Arian Müürisepp - discuss their experience watching the performance.

## Source

## Context

This radio program aired shortly after the premiere of "Võluvärk" (premiered October 8, 2011). It features children's perspectives on the performance.

## Referenced In

This program is cited in Eva-Liisa Linder's Tantsukuukiri article (January 2015) about ZUGA's children's theatre work. Linder quotes children from this program expressing their appreciation for audience participation:

> "Mulle meeldis hirmsasti see, et neil sai ise kaasa teha." (I really liked that we could participate ourselves.)

And one child proudly stating:

> "Ja minu vend sai olla postkast!" (And my brother got to be a mailbox!)

## Significance

- Documents children's authentic reactions to "Võluvärk"
- Demonstrates the impact of participatory theatre elements on young audiences
- Provides primary source evidence of ZUGA's audience engagement approach
- Shows how participation creates memorable experiences for children
