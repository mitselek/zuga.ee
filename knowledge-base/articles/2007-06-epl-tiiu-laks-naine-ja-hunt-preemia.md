# ZUGA "Naine ja hunt" sai taas preemia

**Eesti Päevaleht**
Published: June 4, 2007
Author: Tiiu Laks

Article announcing Philip Morris Eesti tantsuauhinnad 2006 (Philip Morris Estonian Dance Awards 2006), awarded on May 31, 2007.

## Source

https://epl.delfi.ee/artikkel/51089440/zuga-naine-ja-hunt-sai-taas-preemia

## Key Information

### Philip Morris Dance Award 2006

**Winner: ZUGA ühendatud tantsijad**

**Category:** Aasta tegija 2006 (Creator of the Year 2006)

**Performance:** "Naine ja hunt" - production combining movement, word, and video art

**Other nominees:**

- Katrin Essenson and Mari Savitski
- Oksana Titova and Juhan Ulfsak
- Dance theatre Fine 5

### Award Context

This was "Naine ja hunt's" **second major award**:

1. **Spring 2007:** Eesti teatri aastaauhind (Estonian Theatre Annual Award) - Contemporary Dance category
2. **May 31, 2007:** Philip Morris Dance Award 2006 - Creator of the Year

From the article:

> "Kevadel võitis sama lavastus Eesti teatri aastaauhinna nüüdisaegse tantsu kategoorias."

(In spring, the same production won the Estonian Theatre Annual Award in the contemporary dance category.)

### Kaja Kann's Response

Kaja Kann from ZUGA was grateful for the recognition but noted that the Philip Morris dance awards jury could have made a different decision than the Estonian Theatre Annual Awards jury.

> "Kaja Kann ZUGA-st oli küll tänulik tunnustuse eest, kuid leidis, et Philip Morrise tantsuauhindade žürii oleks võinud teha Eesti teatri aastaauhindade žüriist erineva otsuse."

### Other Awards

**Aasta läbilöögi auhind 2006** (Breakthrough of the Year 2006)

**Winner:** Triin Reemann

**Performance:** "Hea kuudis" (diploma work from Tartu Ülikooli Viljandi Kultuurikolledž)

**Other nominees:**

- Sandra Z
- Laura Kvelstein and Dmitri Kruus

Note: Reemann's duet with Silver Elves won third prize at an international choreography competition in Germany in March.

### Jury Members

- Eve Noormets (Noore Tantsu Festival, Viljandi)
- Anu Tähemaa (Sõltumatu Tantsu Ühendus)
- Priit Raud (Kanuti Gildi SAAL)
- Helena Ehrenbusch (Eesti tantsukunstnike liit)
- Katrin Ruus (Eclectica festival, Tartu)

## Awards Summary for "Naine ja Hunt"

1. **Eesti teatri aastaauhind** (Spring 2007) - Contemporary Dance category
2. **Philip Morris Dance Award 2006** (May 31, 2007) - Creator of the Year
3. **Tantsuauhind 2007** (Eesti Teatriliit) - Idea, choreography, and video direction

Three major awards for one production in 2007!
