## Dance performance NOISE (MÜRA)

**Helena Krinal/ZUGA**

In a world where noise has become the norm, it is the silence that terrifies us. 'Noise' is a physical meeting of sounds, bodies, space and vibrations; soaked and served with all kinds of noise we have grown accustomed to, regardless of their disruptive effect on our lives. A dance performance born in the quiet of Estonia and raised on the loud streets of Palestine.

**Director:** Helena Krinal (krinal | mansour)

**Performers:** Tiina Mölder, Helen Reitsnik ja Ajjar Ausma

**Set designer:** Keili Retter

**Light designer:** Oliver Kulpsoo

**Sound and graphic designer:** Asma Ghanem (Palestiina)

**Dramaturg:** Katrin Tegova

**Production:** Väike Objekt A and United Dancers of Zuga

**Co-production:** krinal | mansour and Sõltumatu Tantsu Lava

**Premiere:** 6.06.2019 at Sõltumatu Tantsu Lava in Tallinn, Estonia
