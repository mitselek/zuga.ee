# Zuga - Noise

Director: Helena Krinal (krinal | mansour) Performers: Tiina Mölder, Helen Reitsnik and Ajjar Ausma Set designer: Keili Retter Light designer: Oliver Kulpsoo Sound and graphic designer: Asma Ghanem (Palestine) Dramaturg: Katrin Tegova Production: Väike Objekt A and United Dancers of

![](https://web.archive.org/web/20250125115216im_/https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/YR0RQzXtIXH6i-htlpmseejFRxy5fWy8mSnJAz14pp_Vrv-MVTyX3Ou8ha5jT_qrb2N_f_bsjVBHycHG3GDpWvpGG4JljLCIA1jXwfriFhOS6kR7=w1280)

In a world where noise has become the norm, it is the silence that terrifies us.

‘Noise’ is a physical meeting of sounds, bodies, space and vibrations; soaked and served with all kinds of noise we have grown accustomed to, regardless of their disruptive effect on our lives.

A dance performance born in the quiet of Estonia and raised on the loud streets of Palestine.
